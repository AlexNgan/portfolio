Biography

Hi, I'm a first year engineering student at the Rensselear Polytechnic Institute.

I'm a New York native, born in Manhattan, raised in Queens and the Bronx. As such, I've got a passion for improving the public transportation and accessibility.

My hobbies include writing, graphic design, and programming. I've also got a lot of background in CAD and mechanical design with FRC robotics, both as a student and as a mentor.


What is TITAN DESIGNS?

TITAN is my personal design brand; this website is both a personal website as well as an online portfolio. All in all, this is a snapshot of my foray into design.
