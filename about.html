Biography

Hi, I'm a second year undergraduate student at the Rensselear Polytechnic Institute. Currently, I'm working towards a dual degree in Communication and Design, Innovation, and Society, a unique combination of engineering and disciplines in the liberal arts, including social science and design.

I intend to apply to a veterinary school after my Bachelor's. I hope to use these skills to design prosthetics for animals.

I'm a New York native, born in Manhattan, and grew up in Queens and the Bronx. As such, I've got a passion for improving the public transportation and accessibility, having spent a great deal of my life riding around in buses and subways.

My hobbies include drawing, programming, writing, and graphic design. The former two were developed a great deal during my time as an editor for my high school's newspaper, The Science Survey. I've also got a lot of background in CAD and mechanical design with FRC robotics, both as a student and as a mentor.
