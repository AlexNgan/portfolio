Biography

>> [PDF] Resume

I'm graduating from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a BS in Communication and Design, Innovation, and Society, a unique combination of engineering and disciplines in the liberal arts, including social science and design.

The DIS program focuses on social design—that is, not just how we can create something, but also why we choose to design a product a certain way. As an engineer, I’m fascinated in product design and dynamic solutions for complex issues. As such, I’m interested in working with companies that delve into innovation. After completing my undergraduate degree, I hope to apply my skills in a field where I can better the quality of people’s lives.

I'm a New York native, born in Manhattan, and grew up in Queens and the Bronx. As such, I've got a passion for improving the public transportation and accessibility, having spent a great deal of my life riding around in buses and subways.

My hobbies include drawing, programming, writing, and graphic design. The former two were developed a great deal during my time as an editor for my high school's newspaper, The Science Survey. I've also got a lot of background in CAD and mechanical design with FRC robotics, both as a student and as a mentor.
