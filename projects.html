Historical Design Series | Fall 2018

Operating off a design brief, I was constrained with the task of emulating the style of prominent ceramics artist Eva Zeisel.

This model was entirely created from a series of sketches and ultimately modeled in a CAD program. Comprised of a ceramic body and a glass base, this coffee pot invokes Zeisel's elegant design while incorporating pour-over components for brewing freshly ground coffee beans.
