Hi, I'm Alex.

I'm a student at Rensselear Polytechnic, Class of '20. Design, Innovation, and Society and Communication.

Personally, while I feel that design is a tool to address issues, a great deal of what it can do is rooted in the philosophy behind a product. Without a deeper understanding of the problem, most "solutions" are just bandaid responses to a symptom. At the end of the day, pretty much everyone can design and problem-solve, but a good designer is someone who can create a conversation with their work.
