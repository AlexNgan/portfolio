Hi, I'm Alex.

I'm a student at Rensselear Polytechnic, Class of '20. Design, Innovation, and Society and Communication.

Currently, I’m working on my senior capstone, which focuses on expanding options for transgender and gender non-comforming individuals within the sex toy market. While this space has become less destigmatized, there is still a great amount of taboo surrounding both transgender bodies and sexual pleasure. This project is in progress, but I aim to design a series of produsts for pleasure by consolidating knowledge, experience, and unmet needs from the greater community of queer and gender non-conforming individuals.

Personally, while I feel that design is a tool to address issues, a great deal of what it can do is rooted in the philosophy behind a product. Without a deeper understanding of the problem, most "solutions" are just bandaid responses to a symptom. At the end of the day, pretty much everyone can design and problem-solve, but a good designer is someone who can create a conversation with their work.
